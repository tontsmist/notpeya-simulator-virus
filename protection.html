🛡️ How to Protect Yourself from NotPetya

Protecting yourself from malware like NotPetya involves proactive digital hygiene:

  • 💾 Backup Your Data: Keep secure backups offline or in the cloud.
  • 🔒 Use Antivirus: Install and update trusted antivirus software.
  • 📥 Update Systems: Always keep your OS and software up to date.
  • 📧 Don’t Open Suspicious Emails: Avoid clicking unknown links or attachments.
  • 🌐 Use Firewalls: Restrict unauthorized access with firewalls.
  • 👤 Least Privilege: Use limited user accounts for daily activities.

NotPetya exploited outdated Windows vulnerabilities. Patch management and user awareness are key!
